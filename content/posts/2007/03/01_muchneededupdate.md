It has been a long time since wrote in my blog, mostly because life has been busy, and when it's not, I have been lazy. I have tried not being, but somehow I always succumb to it, much to my dislike, but all we can do is try.

Anyway, where do I even begin? A lot has happened. Let me first being with gaming.

I started playing Hitman Blood Money a while, but I uninstalled the game because I cannot save during a mission and then quit the game. If I do, I would have to begin the game from the beginning again. I usually don't have the time to play and finish one mission at a stretch, so I am going leave this game for later.

After much thought, I decided I am going to pick Commandos 2 again. I have played this game a long time ago, finished a couple of missions but left it after that. I cannot remember why I did that. So I started playing it right from the start again. It's an amazing game, which has improved a lot over the previous two versions (Commandos Behind Enemy Lines and Commandos Beyond the Call of Duty). I have finished both those games, and I found them to be insanely difficult. This game isn't that difficult and progressing through the missions is even. But I am not going to review this game right now, once I am through with it, I will write the review.

I have finished a couple of games recently, Soldier of Fortune 2 Double Helix and Unreal Tournament 2003. Both are very solid games, as most of you all would already know. I haven't started working on the reviews yet...I should in fact.

I also set up dual monitors in my room, just for the sake of experimenting. Nvidia has excellent driver support for dual monitors. I don't know a whole lot about other video cards, but Nvidia has made it extremely easy to configure and use dual monitors. I am using one monitor regular work, while the other I use for watching movies only. The other monitor is closer to the bed, so I can play a movie and lay back on my bed watch it, instead of sitting in front of the computer - which is much awkward comparatively.

I should make it a point to update my blog at least once a week, lest my friends should lose interest in me! I have become extremely disorganized over the past few weeks...I don't know even what I did with my time. I played games of course, but I did that for years, so nothing new there, but apart from that, I don't know how the time has passed. I have become a chronic procrastinator! But I will try my best to mend my ways.

I want to thank my friends for being patient with me. It's you guys that make GameSpot special, and not just another gaming site, you have my gratitude.

Now, I better go read all your blogs! See you around!
