Hello all,

It took longer than I expected for me to be back here. Let me update you all on everything that has happened over the last few weeks.

###### Exams

The gist of the matter is of course my exams. I am happy to announce that I did fairly well, and I should pass with decent marks. I am quite relieved with the way the exam situation went, because it could have been a lot worse. The problem was, I had 5 exams in 5 days, from 9AM-12PM. Some of the subjects I had this semester were quite difficult, so I didn't get to sleep well during the course of the exams. I might have slept for about 2-3 hours each day. My body cycle was disturbed because I had to sleep right after my exam and study through the night. I had slight fever for my last exam, but I wrote the exam better than I thought I would, so I am quite pleased with the outcome.

###### Cleaning

After my exams, I immediately started with the room cleaning process. I was quite determined to get rid of all the junk that accumulated for years. The whole process took me 4 days to complete, during which time my computer was dismantled, so I couldn't get online.

Apart from just cleaning, I also wanted to make my room feel pleasant. So I took off the old curtains, and bought new ones. I love the way my room looks now; I think they match the off white color of the walls very well.

I also removed some unwanted pieces of furniture, so my room is very spacious and it is now my sanctuary where I can relax without being disturbed. I think I needed a place like this after a hectic time during my exams.

I will post old and new pictures of my room. I just need to get my hands on a digital camera.

###### Games

As you all know (or at least I think you do), I have been playing No One Lives Forever 2 for a while now. I managed to finish it yesterday. I loved the game and I recommend it to all PC gamers. I heard that the console version of the game isn't good - yet another example where cross platform ports don't live up to the standards. I am going to work on the review soon, I just need to find a little time for that, I have a lot of correspondence I need to tend to before I can get to review the game.

As of now, I started playing Half Life Blue Shift. I was hoping to finish this game in a day or two at the maximum, but I don't think that is possible, I have a very busy day ahead of me, but I think I should be done with it by the weekend if time permits.

###### Closing

That's about all for now; I am looking forward to getting a camera so I can post pictures. I hope you all have been doing well. See you around!
