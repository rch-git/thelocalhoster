I am not uspposed to be here until after May 20th...supposed to be studying, but life never goes on as we want it to, right? So yeah, in spite of having an exam tomorrow, I am here updating my blog. Compulsive? I don't know. Bored out of my skin? Certainly.

Well, when I am here, I may as well be cheerful. There is no point in whining too much. We all have problems, so nothing new there.

I have just discovered that the demo of Desperados 2 is out! Good news indeed. Bad news is, its 406MB. It’s going to take a while to download it. I don’t know if I should download it, or wait until it’s out on DVD which comes along with the monthly computer magazines.

Here in India, we don’t have a lot of gaming magazines, so getting hold of the latest demos is a little difficult. I have not decided yet…I think I will wait until my exams are over to download the demo. It’s very unlikely that the demo of this game would be out next month on DVD.

I haven’t been playing any games lately. Except the demo of Need For Speed Most Wanted – my 7 year old cousin wants me to play it at least once a day.

I am seriously thinking about resuming Caesar 3 again, once my exams are over that is. Two more levels to go before I finish the game…I just want to be done with it. I hate leaving games unfinished.

Hmm…that’s about all for now.
