#### 6:09 PM 8/27/2006

Well, I had see what the fuss was all about, so I finally started playing Half Life. I had this game for over 5 years now, and I played a little bit back then and gave up....I can't remember why. Anyway, I started this game a while ago, and I managed to finish quite a bit till now.

I can imagine why this game became so popular back in 1999. The graphics were really good for its time, after all it's the Quake 2 engine we are talking about! The game play is not repetitive, and the level design is great.

The story is really complex though, I am yet to read the whole thing, but I will sometime soon. I found this fantastic site, which has the entire story of the Half Life series so far.

Link (http://members.shaw.ca/halflifestory/index.htm)

I am almost at the end of Residue Processing level. Will update this once I am done with it.

#### 12:47 PM 11/30/2006
