title: 'Creative Speakers'
---
I finally got the speakers that I wanted for a long time now. Creative 5.1.

These speakers are amazing. The previous speakers I had, Intex 4.1 2600W PMPO, were decent, but to get the best out of gaming audio experience, they really were not good enough.

Setting up the speakers is a bit of a task, it took me a while to get everything done. And after configuring the audio driver, I played Green Day's 'Boulevard of broken dreams'. It was something else.

I wonder how it would be like, play Doom 3 on my PC now, with dim lights in my room. It's going to be pretty scary, that's for sure.
