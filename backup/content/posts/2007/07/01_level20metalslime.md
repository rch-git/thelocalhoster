#### Games

As you all can see on my Xfire tag, I am still playing Deus Ex. I didn't know the game would take this long to finish. I also didn't know there was so much depth in the gameplay. I have never previously played any RPG games, and so I made some blunders with the skill points initially, but that didn't cost me too much. Now I am more cautious and prudent with the skill points that I acquire.

If there are any FPS fans out there that haven't played this game then give this game a shot. You don't get to play games like this every day. It's not your average FPS game, and the visuals are a bit dull but you'll soon forget that once you get into the gameplay, which is very smooth and keeps you riveted.

No reviews this time around. I didn't know what to review actually. I am thinking about reviewing all the Tomb Raider games that I have played, from 1 to 5. I have written a Tomb Raider 3 review already, so I am looking to work on the other titles.
