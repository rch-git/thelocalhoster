#### 3/11/2006 9:33:58 PM

As of now, I am trying to balance between studying, gaming, exercise, social life, and internet life. I really need to prioritize everything.

I have exams next week and as ever I have no inclination to study, I don’t know what's gotten into me; I didn’t have this problem before. I hope I can work it out somehow.

I didn’t make too much use of today, I should have studied a lot more than I did, but then, I did get a start, and looking at things on the bright side, if I should carry on from here, I don’t have too much to worry about.

I hope, I can make tonight count. Because I got up pretty late today, I want to study through the night – it’s something I have done in the past, but not recently. I need to get back to old habits of studying for long hours.

I have become quite lazy over the last few months – not good.

I need to exercise as well. I did my share of crunches today, so I am happy about that, and I want to keep it going. Consistency and planning are the key, I feel. I hope I can get it right.

Like I said, I want to study hard, exercise hard, and play harder! Good luck to me :D
