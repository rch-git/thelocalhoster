#### 10:48 PM 3/10/2006

Currently playing GTA 3. I finished 'Espresso-2-go' missions today. Two more missions to go till the end of the game. I think I played this gamer for about 2 months, not too sure, but it's certainly addictive.

This is one of those few games which have a very high replay value. Some of the missions are quite frustrating, at least that's what I felt, but over all, this happens to be a great game.

Sure enough, it has a lot of voilence, and it's certainly not for everyone, because there is offensive language, but in a way, it's only appropriate.

All in all, I think the makers got everything just right. And as the popular saying goes, you are only as voilent as you want to be.
